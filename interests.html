  • Video Games:
    • The Last of Us and The Last of Us Part II
      These games have some of the best level design out of all the games I have played and is an all-time favourite of mine. The level design in these games is what I aspire to create. The sound design is also incredible, as well as all the details in the environmental storytelling. I hope to put the same amount of detail in the games that I will create one day.
    • Horizon Zero Dawn
      The story and world building is original, incredible and immersive. The enemy designs are unique and the combat is very fun. Even after finishing the story it was fun to complete all the side quests which fleshed out the world and characters.
    • The Uncharted Series
      These are some of my favourite games. Naughty Dog consistently creates beautiful stories and worlds that capture the player. The level design is also incredible in these games.
    • Overwatch
      Overwatch has excellent level design, character design, and sound design, which creates an engaging and fast paced team shooter which keeps me coming back to play the game after all these years.
